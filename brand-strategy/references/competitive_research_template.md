## Dimensions (30 data points per competitor)

For each dimension, provide a clear, concise answer. If information is not publicly available, write "Информация не найдена". Do NOT skip any dimension.

### A. Identification (3 dimensions)
1. **competitor_name** — Full Russian name
2. **competitor_type** — "Bank-affiliated broker" / "Independent broker" / "Boutique/HNWI broker"
3. **parent_company** — Parent bank/group, or "Independent"

### B. Brand & Positioning (5 dimensions)
4. **brand_essence** — Core brand promise in 1-2 sentences. Quote their actual tagline/slogan if available (in «кавычках»).
5. **brand_positioning** — Market positioning: mass-market, premium, tech-forward, expert, accessible, etc. Describe implied positioning toward investors.
6. **brand_archetype** — Dominant archetype (Hero, Sage, Creator, Magician, Explorer, Ruler, Caregiver, Regular Guy, Rebel, etc.) with 1-sentence justification.
7. **brand_image** — Visual identity: logo, primary colors (hex if possible), design language, aesthetic (corporate/modern/playful/premium/minimalist).
8. **tone_of_voice** — Communication tone: formal/informal, expert/accessible, aggressive/soft, inspirational/practical. Include 1-2 verbatim examples from their ads or social media.

### C. Communication & Channels (4 dimensions)
9. **communication_channels** — All known channels: TV, digital (programmatic, search, display), social media platforms, outdoor, content marketing, events, sponsorships, influencer partnerships.
10. **social_media_presence** — Key accounts with follower counts where available. Cover: Telegram, VK, YouTube, Instagram, Дзен, TikTok.
11. **content_and_education** — Educational products: analytics platforms, investment ideas services, webinars, courses, media brands, podcasts, blogs. Name specific products (e.g., "Т-Журнал", "Школа инвестора Сбер", "ФИНАМ.ру медиа").
12. **key_messages** — 3-5 recurring themes in 2024-2026 marketing. What do they repeatedly communicate?

### D. Product & Service (5 dimensions)
13. **product_range** — Investment instruments: акции, облигации, ОФЗ, ETF/ПИФы, structured products, IPO/pre-IPO, forex, crypto, precious metals, foreign market access, ДУ (trust management), ИИС. Rate breadth: narrow / moderate / broad / full-cycle.
14. **tariff_plans** — Named tiers with key commission rates. Example format: "«Инвестор» — 0.3% от сделки; «Трейдер» — 0.015% от сделки; «Премиум» — индивидуально".
15. **advisory_model** — Financial advice delivery: robo-advisor, personal financial advisor (ФС), AI-driven, self-service, hybrid. Note minimum portfolio thresholds for personal advisory.
16. **affluent_offering** — **CRITICAL DIMENSION. Research thoroughly.** Specific products/services for clients with 2M+ RUB: private banking tiers, premium service levels, exclusive products, dedicated advisors, family office, trust management (ДУ), structured products, pre-IPO access, foreign market access, concierge services, premium app features, offline offices/clubs. If competitor has NO visible affluent offering — state this explicitly as a finding.
17. **unique_features** — 3-5 differentiators unique to this competitor.

### E. Affluent Deep Dive (3 dimensions) — EXPANDED RESEARCH
18. **affluent_entry_threshold** — Minimum asset amount for premium/affluent service tiers. List all tiers with thresholds (e.g., "Premium: от 3 млн ₽; Private: от 30 млн ₽; Ultra: от 100 млн ₽").
19. **affluent_service_model** — How the affluent client is served: dedicated advisor, team model, digital-first with advisor overlay, branch-based, invitation-only. Describe the actual client journey for a 5M+ RUB client.
20. **affluent_brand_communication** — How does the competitor specifically communicate with/about the affluent segment? Separate sub-brand? Dedicated landing pages? Different tone? Different channels? Or same mass-market communication for all? Provide URLs of affluent-specific pages if found.

### F. Digital Experience (3 dimensions)
21. **app_name_and_rating** — Mobile app name, App Store / Google Play ratings, approximate download count.
22. **platform_features** — Key capabilities: trading terminal, screeners, portfolio analytics, social/copy trading, paper trading, AI features, market data, alerts, educational integration.
23. **digital_ux_reputation** — Public perception from reviews: common praise, common complaints, overall sentiment. Is the app best-in-class, adequate, or problematic?

### G. Ecosystem & Integration (2 dimensions)
24. **ecosystem_integration** — Integration with broader ecosystem: bank accounts, cards, cashback on investments, insurance, mortgage, salary projects, single-app experience, cross-sell mechanics.
25. **loyalty_program** — Investment-related loyalty: cashback on commissions, status tiers, referral bonuses, premium banking perks for investors.

### H. Market Position (4 dimensions)
26. **market_share_data** — Assets under management, client count, MOEX ranking, trading volumes. Use most recent data (2025-2026 preferred). Flag data vintage.
27. **target_audience** — Primary target: mass market, mass affluent, HNWI, traders, passive investors, beginners, specific demographics. Describe their ideal client.
28. **competitive_advantages** — 3-5 key advantages vs. other Russian brokers.
29. **competitive_weaknesses** — 3-5 known weaknesses from public reviews, research, market perception.

### I. Source Attribution (1 dimension)
30. **sources** — Semicolon-separated list of key URLs/sources used for this competitor's data. Include: official website, app store link, key media articles, MOEX rankings page. Minimum 3 sources per competitor.

## Research Guidelines

### Source Priority
1. **Official websites** of each competitor (highest priority)
2. **MOEX official data** (moex.com) — rankings, client statistics
3. **Central Bank of Russia** (cbr.ru) — license registry, regulatory data
4. **App stores** — App Store / Google Play ratings and reviews
5. **Industry media** — Frank RG, banki.ru, investfunds.ru, vc.ru, rbc.ru
6. **Social media accounts** — direct observation of tone, content, follower counts
7. **Company press releases and annual reports**

### Quality Requirements
1. **Cross-reference**: 2-3 sources per factual claim minimum
2. **Recency**: 2025-2026 data preferred. Flag explicitly if data is from 2024 or earlier
3. **Specificity**: Not "has a mobile app" → "приложение «Т-Инвестиции», рейтинг 4.7/5 App Store, 10M+ загрузок, известно удобным UX и social trading"
4. **Verbatim quotes**: Include actual slogans, taglines, marketing copy in «кавычках»
5. **Gap documentation**: If a competitor has no visible affluent offering or missing data — state this explicitly. Absence of data IS data.
6. **No hallucination**: If you cannot find information with reasonable confidence, write "Информация не найдена". Do NOT fabricate data.
